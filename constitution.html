Article I - Formation and Membership
Preamble

We the people of the Tundra Fantasy Football League, in order to form a more perfect League, establish justice, ensure fairness, promote a general code of ethics, and secure the blessings of liberty to ourselves and our friends, do ordain and establish this Constitution for the Tundra Fantasy Football League.

Article I – Formation and Membership
Section 1: Formation
              1.1: The name of the League shall be the Tunda Fantasy Football League, hereinafter referred to as the “the League.”
              1.2: For historical purposes, the League shall be recognized as being formed for the 2011 NFL Football Season.
Section 2: Membership
              2.1: The composition of membership for the League shall be twelve (12) members.
              2.2: The entry fee to the League shall be determined each year by a plurality of the League members. The current entry fee is $50.00 USD. Members of the League may vote to increase this fee during the voting period.
              2.3: The deadline for payment of the entry fee is on draft day. If a member fails to tender the payment on this deadline, the League reserves the right to revoke the members 1st round draft pick. Further delay of the payment will result in disciplinary action and eventually termination.
                    2.3.1: Payment must be tendered in cash, there are no IOU’s or “I’ll pay it later”. Members who choose to draft remotely must make prior arrangements with the League in order to deliver their payment in a timely fashion.
              2.4: Ownership of a membership lies in the hands of the League. A member may not sell or pass on their rights to ownership to anyone. If a member wishes to leave the League, they shall report, in writing, their decision to the Commissioner, who will in turn alert the other members of the League and begin to contact individuals outside the League in search of a replacement. Generally there will be no refund of the entry fee, unless extreme personal circumstances exist.
